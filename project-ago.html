Ago Responsive Ecommerce Web App

Helping beauty shoppers find the healthiest products and break free from plastic waste.

The Problem

This student project began with an in-depth development of a fictitious brand. My challenge was to design an online store that helps users find products quickly and easily to match their particular needs. The store should prove valuable to those who have difficulty finding what they want in traditional stores, should allow them to shop easily anywhere they can connect to the internet, and the shopping experience should offer features to complement at least three user stories.

I developed an online store brand called ago to help beauty shoppers find non-toxic products packaged in biodegradable packaging. Shoppers can purchase products for their entire family and feel good about the impact they are making on their health, their community, and the planet. My role as UI designer included naming and brand development to user testing and high-fidelity wireframes.

The Design Process

  • Brand Guidelines
  • User story and user flows
  • Low and mid-fidelity wireframes
  • User testing and compiled user feedack
  • High-fidelity wireframes and breakpoints for four screen sizes

Guiding Principles

Single-use plastics are destroying our planet and its inhabitants, and consumers are concerned and looking for alternatives in every spending category. Without urgent action, the future can seem dire, but there are breakthrough technologies available—from algae-based “plastics” to paperboard tubes—that can make a big difference in reducing waste and toxicity in our environment. The trouble is finding tried and trusted, high-quality products in just one shop.

Ago is a beauty ecommerce site where every product is certified non-toxic and offered in 100% biodegradable packaging.

Ago is committed to offering high-quality, healthy, and affordable skincare and beauty products that reduce environmental impact and disease. From free samples to fast shipping and free returns, it’s never been easier for consumers to make sustainable choices that are better for their families and the planet.
